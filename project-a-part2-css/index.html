[ DiPortal-Fn3 ]

Scientific Name:

DiPortal-Fn3

Discovered by / at:

All human on planet, at 11:00 pm on 14/10/2024.

Family:

DiPortal-Fnalities

Habitat:

Dormant in the 3D Universe.

Appearance:

Can't be seen unless it chooses to appear itself. An enormous hollow hole from its appearance. Transparent at the very beginning.

Key Characteristics:

  • Able to swallow in everything that put into it.
  • Expand gradually as it swallow in more things.
  • Love to be in the dark.
  • Come from another high-dimensional universe.
  • A loyal soldier from that universe.

What we know so far about DiPortal-Fn3? At first nobody recgonized it, all of a sudden it appeared and began to eat up the whole three-dimentional universe, wiping out the universe itself. The creature comes from a higher-dimensional universe, and serves as a loyal soldier to eradicate the lower-dimensional universe in their universe. They love to dormant in these lower dimensions, and once it finished observing, the brutal wipe-out began. The original universe would be brought to their universe as a “printed canvas”.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Press to create planets, and press the mouse constantly to watch it burn and swallowed. Then clicked the certain key to watch the planets come to their universe.
